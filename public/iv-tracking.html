Intrinsic Value Tracking

Métrica institucional — el price-vs-IV gap es lo que determina si una posición es atractiva, no el TWRR. Cada entry post-earnings actualiza EPV o IRR del modelo Columbia y registra el signal zone resultante.

Signal Zones — EPV-driven (gap = (EPV − Price) / Price): Fat Pitch >+30%   Buy/Hold +10 a +30%   Fair ±10%   Expensive −10 a −30%   Bubble <−30%

Signal Zones — IRR-driven (gap = IRR − hurdle 12%): Fat Pitch >+5pp   Buy/Hold 0 a +5pp   Fair −2 a 0pp   Expensive −5 a −2pp   Bubble <−5pp
